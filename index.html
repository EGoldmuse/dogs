Different Dog Breeds

There are many dog breeds in the world but my favorite ones are German Shepard, Siberian Husky, and Golden Retriever.

German Shepard

  • Life expectancy: 9-13 years
  • Origin: Germany
  • Weight: Male: 66-68 lbs, Female: 49-71 lbs
  • Height: Male: 24-26 inches, Female: 22-24 inches

Siberian Husky

  • Life expectancy: 12-15 years
  • Origin: Siberia
  • Weight: Male: 44-60 lbs, Female: 35-51 lbs
  • Height: Male: 21-24 inches, Female: 20-22 inches

Golden Retriever

  • Life expectancy: 10-12 years
  • Origin: United Kingdom, Scottland, England
  • Weight: Male: 65-75 lbs, Female: 55-71 lbs
  • Height: Male: 22-24 inches, Female: 20-22 inches
